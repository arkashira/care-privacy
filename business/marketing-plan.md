### 6. Success Metrics (90‑day targets)  

| Metric | Target (by D+90) | Rationale |
|--------|------------------|-----------|
| **Daily Active Users (DAU)** | **≥ 2,500** (trial + paid) | Indicates product stickiness; ~30 % conversion from trial to paid. |
| **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** | **$250 k** (≈5 × $50 k contracts) | Covers CAC payback within 3 months; validates pricing. |
| **Lead‑to‑Demo Conversion** | **15 %** | From gated assets to scheduled demos. |
| **Demo‑to‑Close Rate** | **20 %** | Reflects strong product‑market fit in regulated sectors. |
| **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** | **≥ 80** (beta & early customers) | Guarantees referral pipeline. |
| **Churn (first 30 days)** | **< 5 %** | Early‑stage retention benchmark. |

*If any metric falls > 20 % short of target, trigger a rapid‑iteration sprint (2‑week) to adjust messaging, pricing, or product features.*
