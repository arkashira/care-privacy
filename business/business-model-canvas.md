# Business Model Canvas

## Customer Segments
- Healthcare institutions (hospitals, clinics, telemedicine providers) - 40% of target market
- Educational institutions (universities, schools, training centers) - 35% of target market  
- Healthcare technology vendors and AI developers - 15% of target market
- Government health and education agencies - 10% of target market
- Individual patients and students seeking privacy control - 5% of target market

## Value Propositions
- Automated consent management reducing manual overhead by 80% for healthcare providers
- Real-time compliance monitoring with automated audit trails for regulatory adherence
- Multi-sector data protection framework supporting HIPAA, GDPR, and local Thai regulations
- Self-service portal enabling patients/students to manage their own data permissions
- API-first architecture allowing seamless integration with existing EHR/LMS systems

## Channels
- Direct sales team targeting healthcare IT procurement departments
- Partner ecosystem including EHR vendors, learning management system providers
- Online marketing through healthcare and education industry publications
- Industry conferences and trade shows in Bangkok, Singapore, and regional markets
- Strategic partnerships with Thai healthcare technology incubators and universities

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated account managers for enterprise clients (healthcare systems, universities)
- Self-service portal for individual users and small practices
- Managed services for compliance monitoring and consent updates
- Community support forums for technical questions and best practices sharing
- Regular customer success calls and quarterly business reviews

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription licensing fees: $2,500-$15,000/month per organization (THB 100,000-600,000)
- Implementation services: $15,000-$50,000 per deployment (THB 600,000-2,000,000)
- Compliance consulting: $200-$500/hour (THB 8,000-20,000)
- Data protection audit services: $5,000-$20,000 per assessment (THB 200,000-800,000)
- Premium support packages: $500-$2,000/month (THB 20,000-80,000)

## Key Resources
- Proprietary consent management engine and data protection algorithms
- Regulatory compliance database covering 15+ jurisdictions including Thailand
- Healthcare and education sector domain expertise team
- Cloud infrastructure with multi-region data residency capabilities
- Strategic partnerships with Thai healthcare technology organizations

## Key Activities
- Platform development and continuous improvement of consent management features
- Regulatory compliance research and adaptation to evolving healthcare privacy laws
- Customer onboarding and implementation support for enterprise clients
- Marketing and sales activities targeting healthcare IT decision-makers
- Ongoing customer support and relationship management

## Key Partners
- Thai healthcare technology incubators and accelerators
- Major EHR vendors (e.g., Medisafe, HealthOne) for co-selling opportunities
- Educational technology platforms (LMS providers) for cross-promotion
- Legal firms specializing in healthcare privacy law for compliance consulting
- Thai Ministry of Health and Ministry of Education for policy alignment

## Cost Structure
- Development and maintenance of core platform: 45% of total costs (THB 18,000,000 annually)
- Sales and marketing operations: 25% of total costs (THB 10,000,000 annually)
- Customer support and implementation services: 20% of total costs (THB 8,000,000 annually)
- Regulatory compliance and legal fees: 8% of total costs (THB 3,200,000 annually)
- Infrastructure and cloud hosting: 2% of total costs (THB 800,000 annually)
