### Narrative Flow (Quick‑Read)

1. **Aware** – A regulator‑driven alarm pushes the CIO to search for solutions. We capture attention with bite‑size compliance content and a free checklist.  
2. **Consider** – The privacy officer downloads the checklist, then explores an interactive sandbox that instantly shows how consent can be captured on a sample record.  
3. **Try** – A 30‑day pilot is launched with pre‑built connectors and a hands‑on workshop, reducing integration time from weeks to days.  
4. **Adopt** – Successful pilot leads to enterprise‑wide rollout, supported by automated policy versioning and quarterly ROI reports.  
5. **Expand** – New AI products trigger additional consent needs; we upsell modular add‑ons and a consent‑orchestrator API, turning compliance into a growth engine.

**Key KPI Stack**

- **Top‑of‑Funnel:** Checklist download CTR ≥ 12 %  
- **Middle‑of‑Funnel:** Sandbox sign‑ups → Qualified Lead conversion ≥ 25 %  
- **Trial Success:** 14‑day “consent captured” milestone ≥ 80 % of pilots  
- **Adoption:** NRR ≥ 110 % within 6 months  
- **Expansion:** Avg. add‑on revenue per account ≥ 30 % of base ARR  

These metrics guide the sales & success teams to monitor health at each stage and continuously iterate on delight opportunities.
